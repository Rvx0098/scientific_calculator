# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to keep this project respectful, constructive, and welcoming for contributors of all experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

- Communicate clearly and respectfully
- Give constructive feedback
- Stay focused on the project
- Respect different skill levels and perspectives

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discriminatory comments
- Personal attacks
- Sharing private information without consent
- Spam or intentionally disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior does not meet these standards.
